Concerts and Event Production

Samesq Productions specializes in all things music. With our successful events in the past such as School Cultural Music Fest 2021 and A December to Remember- All Goa Carol Singing Competition, we can ensure a smooth conduct of competitions, music meets or concerts. Our team oversees all operations from start to finish.

Music Production

Our team produces records and albums for not just our bands like Simple Truth, Shockblast & Dirty Sack, but also jingles and corporate plays for many prestigious organisations. Apart from this, production for School Annual Days and other functions is also managed.

Corporate Events

Corporate Events, Theme Parties, Special Occasion Parties etcetera are our forte. We are committed towards bringing our client's vision to life. From ideation to execution, our team ensures the best services at every step of the way.
